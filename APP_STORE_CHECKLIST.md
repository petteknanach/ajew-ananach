## ⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

### iOS Specific:
- **2.1 App Completeness**: App must be complete
- **4.2 Minimum Functionality**: App must do something useful
- **5.1.1 Data Collection**: Must have privacy policy
- **3.1.1 In-App Purchases**: If free, no hidden purchases

### Android Specific:
- **Policy Violation**: Deceptive behavior
- **Content Policy**: Inappropriate content
- **IP Infringement**: Copyright issues
- **Device and Network Abuse**: Performance issues
