### **Full Description**
**Ajew Ananach brings the complete Breslov library to your mobile device.**

Access 1,000+ teachings from Rabbi Nachman of Breslov and his disciple Rabbi Natan, beautifully presented in a modern, intuitive app.

**📚 Complete Breslov Library:**
- Likutey Moharan (282 chapters)
- Likutey Halachos (600+ teachings)
- Likutey Tefilos (32 prayers)
- Stories & Advice
- Sefer Hamidos
- And many more works

**🔍 Advanced Search:**
Search across all teachings in Hebrew or English. Find wisdom on any topic - joy, prayer, faith, happiness, and more.

**📖 Bilingual Reading:**
Read each teaching in Hebrew or English with a simple toggle. Perfect for learners and native speakers alike.

**⭐ Daily Wisdom:**
Get a new inspirational teaching every day with reflection questions to help apply the wisdom to your life.

**🔖 Personal Library:**
Bookmark your favorite teachings, track your reading progress, and create your personal spiritual journey.

**🎨 Beautiful Design:**
Clean, intuitive interface designed for peaceful reading and spiritual reflection.

**🌐 Connected Community:**
Part of the ajew.org ecosystem, connecting you to Breslov teachings worldwide.

**🔒 Privacy First:**
No ads. No tracking. Just pure wisdom.

**Perfect for:**
- Students of Breslov teachings
- Jewish spiritual seekers
- Hebrew learners
- Anyone looking for daily inspiration
- Those exploring Jewish wisdom

**Na Nach Nachma Nachman Me'Uman:**
This app continues the mission of spreading the light of Rebbe Nachman's teachings to every Jew, everywhere.
