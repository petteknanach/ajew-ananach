🦞 Privacy Policy - Ajew Ananach

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Introduction

Ajew Ananach ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our service:

  • App Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to understand how features are used
  • Device Information: Basic device type and OS version for compatibility
  • Local Storage: Your bookmarks, reading progress, and preferences (stored only on your device)
  • Search Queries: Search terms stay on your device and are not sent to our servers

3. How We Use Information

  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand which features are most useful
  • Ensure compatibility with different devices
  • Never for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Never sold or shared with third parties

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your bookmarks and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure storage
  • We cannot access your personal reading data
  • Analytics data is anonymous and aggregated
  • We use industry-standard security practices

5. Third-Party Services

We use these services to improve the app:

  • Expo: For app development and deployment (anonymous usage data)
  • Firebase: For crash reporting (anonymous data only)
  • App Store/Play Store: For app distribution (subject to their privacy policies)

6. Your Rights

  • Access Your Data: All your data is stored locally on your device
  • Delete Your Data: Clear app data in your device settings
  • Opt Out: Disable analytics in app settings
  • Export Your Data: Contact us to export your bookmarks

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@ajew.org
  • Website: https://ajew.org
  • Support: https://ajew.org/support

10. Consent

By using our app, you consent to our Privacy Policy.
