📱 App Issues & Troubleshooting

Common Problems & Solutions

App won't open or crashes
  • Restart your device
  • Update to the latest version of the app
  • Clear app cache (in device settings)
  • Reinstall the app
Content not loading
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try switching between WiFi and mobile data
  • Restart the app
  • Content is cached for offline use once loaded
Search not working
  • Ensure you have the latest app version
  • Search works in both Hebrew and English
  • Try simpler search terms
  • Search index loads on first use

🔧 Technical Support

Contact Technical Support

Email: support@ajew.org

Please include:

  • Device model (iPhone 12, Samsung Galaxy S21, etc.)
  • App version (found in Settings → About)
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Screenshots if possible
  • Steps to reproduce the problem

Response time: 24-48 hours for technical issues

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app really free?
Yes! Completely free, no ads, no in-app purchases. Spreading Breslov wisdom is our mission.
Does it work offline?
Yes! Once you load teachings, they're available offline. Bookmarks and reading progress are saved locally.
Can I suggest features or report errors?
Absolutely! Email: features@ajew.org for feature requests, errors@ajew.org for content errors.
How often is content updated?
New teachings added weekly. App updates monthly with new features and content.
Is my reading data private?
Yes! All your data (bookmarks, progress) stays on your device. We don't track your reading.
